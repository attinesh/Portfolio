Ashish Singh

I am a Ph.D.student in Aerospace Engineering at the Univeristy of Arizona. My primary research focus is on nanosecond dielectric barrier discharge (ns-DBD) plasma actuators. I study the flow physics of these devices through experimental methods such as Particle Image Velocimetry (PIV), Schlieren flow visualization, hot wire anemometry and wake rake measurements.
